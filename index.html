About Us

Krsitan Tayo is a born-and-raised Filipino graphic designer and illustrator living in Vancouver, Canada. Before moving to Canada, Kristian earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a major in Visual Communication in the University of the Philippines-Diliman College of Fine Arts and graduated cum laude. He worked with various clients in the Philippines through the various agencies and corporations he worked for - namely, Huskee Digital Inc., the Design Center of the Philippines, and Edsa Shangri-La, Manila. He won multiple awards throughout his time and he's still doing well.

Kristian earned his Certificate in Graphic Design from the British Columbia in 2025 and went on to work with one of the biggest agencies in Vancouver. He-cofounded a design studio called Trash Panda Design Studios, which aims to uplift small-to-medium enterprises owned by racial minorities and members of the LGBTQ+ community and help them compete with bigger corporations in the same market. He also authored his award-winning graphic novel series, "The Legend of Hyperion", which tells the story of magic, friendship, and what it takes to be a hero. The graphic novel inspired the Emmy-nominated HBO Max fantasy TV series of the same name and had Kristian at the helm as writer and executive producer.
